2 Year anniversary

June 18 2020

We at WWCP are happy to present you the 2 Years anniversary of the Content Pack. A lot has happened throughout the two years, we got a website up running where we will occasionally update news with major updates of the pack. We also got more Coders, Contributors and Artists to help out, huge thanks to them and together we will look forward to more content! We would also like to give a huge thank you out to all our followers for their continious critical feedback and help. But for now Hip Hip Hurray on 2 years!

About us

Content

WWCP is the biggest content pack for Trains in Motion aka the Traincraft rewrite by EternalBlueflame. Our main goal since the start was to grow as organically as possible to feature as many different countries and their respective rolling stock. After nearly 1 and a half years we can already say we have over 7 different countries added and over 30 individual pieces of rolling stock existing in game.
